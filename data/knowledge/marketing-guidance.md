# GMCC Marketing Guidance & Selling Points

## What is CRA Pricing?

CRA (Community Reinvestment Act) requires banks to serve underserved communities. GMCC's CRA programs pass this benefit to borrowers as significantly better pricing — typically 1.5 to 5+ points better than conventional rates.

## Key Selling Points for Realtors

### 1. Better Pricing = Stronger Buyer
- CRA pricing can reduce the interest rate by 0.5-1.5% compared to conventional
- This translates to hundreds of dollars less per month on jumbo loans
- Buyers with CRA-eligible properties can make more competitive offers

### 2. Same Process, Better Rate
- CRA loans follow the same underwriting as conventional — no extra paperwork
- The property location does the work, not the borrower's income (for most programs)
- Appraisal, credit, and closing processes are identical

### 3. No Income Caps on Most Programs
- GMCC Diamond CRA has NO income restriction
- GMCC Jumbo CRA has no income cap
- This is unusual — most CRA programs limit by borrower income

### 4. Geographic Breadth
- Diamond CRA covers all 50 states
- Jumbo CRA covers 20+ counties in 11 states
- Special Conforming covers 16 MSAs across 8 states

## How to Check CRA Eligibility

A property qualifies for CRA pricing based on its census tract designation:
- **LMI (Low-to-Moderate Income) tract** — required by Jumbo CRA, Grandslam
- **MMCT (Majority-Minority Census Tract)** — required by $10K Grant, Special Conforming
- **Eligible census tract list** — required by Diamond CRA

GMCC's search tool automatically checks these designations using FFIEC data.

## Objection Handling

**"I've never heard of CRA loans"**
→ CRA programs have been around for decades, but few lenders pass the pricing benefit to borrowers. GMCC specializes in making CRA incentives available as competitive rates.

**"Sounds too good to be true — what's the catch?"**
→ No catch. The better pricing comes from CRA regulatory incentives that banks receive. GMCC passes these savings directly to borrowers. Same underwriting, same process.

**"My client's income is too high for CRA"**
→ Most GMCC CRA programs have NO income cap. Diamond CRA and Jumbo CRA qualify based on property location (census tract), not borrower income.

**"Is this only for first-time homebuyers?"**
→ No. Diamond CRA, Jumbo CRA, and Special Conforming are available regardless of homeownership history. Only Grandslam has first-time buyer focus.

**"What about refinancing?"**
→ Jumbo CRA supports rate/term and cash-out refinancing. Diamond CRA supports refinancing. Grandslam and $10K Grant are purchase-only.

## Email Tone Guidance

When the AI drafts emails to realtors about CRA programs:
- Lead with the pricing advantage (concrete numbers)
- Mention the specific property and how it qualifies
- Keep it warm but professional — loan officers know their realtors
- Include a specific call to action ("Let's discuss this opportunity for your client")
- Don't use jargon like "FFIEC" or "census tract" — say "this property qualifies for special pricing"
